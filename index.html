Welcome to My Research Portfolio

Welcome to my academic and professional portfolio. Here you'll find a comprehensive overview of my research projects, publications, and professional activities spanning machine learning, infrastructure engineering, and optimization.

I'm passionate about applying cutting-edge computational methods to solve real-world engineering challenges. Feel free to explore my work and don't hesitate to reach out if you'd like to collaborate or discuss any of these projects.

Industry Projects

  • Demand Forecasting for Consumer Goods - Advanced machine learning approaches for predicting consumer demand patterns and optimizing inventory management systems.
  • Planning Maintenance on Transportation Infrastructure - Developing intelligent maintenance scheduling systems using reinforcement learning and probabilistic modeling.
  • Modelling the Deterioration of Infrastructure - Structural health monitoring and deterioration prediction models for large-scale infrastructure networks.
  • Oil Field Development and Planning - Optimization algorithms for well placement and field development strategies in petroleum engineering.

Cloud Applications

  • OpenIPDM Light - Interactive web application for infrastructure probabilistic deterioration modeling and analysis.
  • Concrete Surface Cracks Detection - Computer vision application for automated detection and classification of concrete surface defects.
  • Academic Cheatsheets - Curated collection of academic resources and quick reference guides for researchers and students.
